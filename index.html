Exploratory Testing Peer Conference

Exploratory Testing Peer Conference is a series of international peer to peer learning events by Maaret Pyhäjärvi, Anne-Marie Charrett and Alex Schladebeck, facilitated with the intent of rediscovering Exploratory Testing after 35 years of coining the term.

The peer conference format is very loosely inspired by LAWST-style peer conferences.
